How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

To provide and maintain our services, process your requests, and communicate with you about your inquiries.

Improvement

To improve our website, products, and services based on your feedback and usage patterns.

Communication

To send you updates, newsletters, and promotional materials (with your consent).

Legal Compliance

To comply with legal obligations and protect our legitimate business interests.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • • With service providers who assist in operating our website

  • • With your explicit consent
